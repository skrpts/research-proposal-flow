## Capability

Analyses a body of existing research to identify gaps: questions that remain unanswered, populations that are underrepresented, methodologies that haven't been applied, and contexts that haven't been explored.

## When to Use

- Defining a research question for a new study
- Justifying a research proposal by demonstrating the need
- Identifying opportunities for original contribution
- Reviewing a field to find under-explored areas

## Inputs

Literature review summary or collection of paper summaries, research field description, known limitations from existing studies

## Outputs

Structured gap analysis: identified gaps categorised by type (knowledge gap, methodological gap, population gap, contextual gap), evidence supporting each gap, and suggested research questions that would address them
